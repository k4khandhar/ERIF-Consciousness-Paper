ERIF Theory

A Cognitive Map of Consciousness

ERIF maps consciousness as movement through cognitive space rather than as a static property.

What is ERIF?

ERIF is a cognitive map — a 2D plane where minds evolve with purpose and polarity. Every system (a brain, a chatbot, or even you) can be located by just two forces:

  • T = Stability: The internal drive to stay consistent, grounded
  • R = Integration: The drive to grow, combine signals, and adapt

Imagine consciousness not as a switch or score — but as a dot moving through this space.
